  - Regulation (EU) 2016/679 articles cited by number for each right (Art. 15 access, 16 rectification, 17 erasure, 18 restriction, 20 portability, 21 objection, 22 automated decision-making)
  - Art. 27 EU representative requirement for non-EU controllers — kitsubeat IS a non-EU controller (UK sole trader) and WILL process EU data subjects → this triggers a lawyer-flag unless Art. 27(2) exemption applies (occasional processing, small scale, no special-category data, low risk). Analyze whether kitsubeat qualifies for the exemption and rate.
  - EDPB guidance documents on Art. 27 and breach notification
  - Cookies: ePrivacy Directive 2002/58/EC (transposed nationally — flag the fragmentation) + EDPB cookie guidelines
  - SCCs (2021 Standard Contractual Clauses) for transfer to Supabase US region

- **§2.4 LGPD (Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados, Lei 13.709/2018)**
  - Lawful bases (Art. 7 for general data, Art. 11 for sensitive data)
  - Data subject rights (Art. 18)
  - ANPD (Autoridade Nacional de Proteção de Dados) guidance and breach notification regime (Art. 48 — "reasonable period")
  - Representative in Brazil requirement (Art. 5 XIII — DPO, not territorial rep — but the controller abroad must appoint; cite.)
  - International transfer regime (Art. 33 — adequacy list, SCCs, specific contractual clauses). Brazil's adequacy list is limited; confirm whether US/UK are on it.
  - If LGPD compliance requires a Brazilian representative AND no Art. 27-style small-scale exemption exists, rate this 🔴 and lawyer-flag.

- **§2.5 CCPA (+ CPRA amendments)**

Note that kitsubeat will NOT meet the $25M/100k-consumer thresholds at beta, so CCPA may not technically apply — BUT the parallel-structure treatment still documents it for when it does.
  - Consumer rights (know, delete, correct, opt-out of sale/share, limit use of sensitive PI, non-discrimination)
  - "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" signal — Global Privacy Control (GPC) handling obligation once applicability hits
  - Verifiable consumer request process — 45-day response window + one 45-day extension
